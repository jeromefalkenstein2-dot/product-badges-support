FAQ

How do I add a badge to a product?

Go to the Product Badges dashboard in your Shopify admin, click "Create Badge", select your product, and save. The badge will appear on your product page immediately.

Which themes are supported?

Product Badges works with all Shopify themes that support Online Store 2.0 app blocks.

Will badges slow down my store?

No. Product Badges is built for performance — badges load asynchronously and have no impact on your store's page speed score.

How do I remove a badge?

Open the Product Badges dashboard, find the badge, and click Delete. It will be removed from your store instantly.

Can I use automatic rules?

Yes. With the Pro plan, you can set up automatic rules to apply badges based on sale price, stock level, or product age.
